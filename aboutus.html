Each week’s Sack’s content is different. Vegetables are selected to work together to make complete dishes from the 3-5 recipes we provide with each theme. Highlights may include breads from Baker Street Bread in Chestnut Hill, different PA local cheeses, Greek-style yogurt, and uncommon veggies for you to try!






Our produce is sourced from 50-60 farms in PA -- one of which is as local as Roxborough, grown and harvested from the Schuylkill Center by FNC Philly Farms! If it’s out of season, we find and bring you Certified Organic equivalents. Our goal is to minimize the footprint of how food gets on your table, ensuring freshness and reducing chemical treatments while supporting local farms.
